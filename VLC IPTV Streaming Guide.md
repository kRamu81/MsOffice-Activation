# 📺 VLC IPTV Streaming Guide

Watch thousands of publicly available TV channels and live streams from around the world using VLC Media Player and free IPTV playlists.

> ⚠️ Disclaimer:
> This repository only references **publicly available IPTV playlists**. We do not host, distribute, or promote copyrighted content. Availability depends on the playlist maintainers and broadcasters.

---

## ✨ Features

- 📺 Watch public TV channels
- ⚽ Sports channels
- 📰 News
- 🎬 Movies
- 🎵 Music
- 👶 Kids channels
- 🌍 International TV
- 💻 Works on Windows, Linux, macOS, Android & iOS

---

# 📋 Requirements

- VLC Media Player
- Internet Connection
- Public IPTV Playlist URL

---

# 🚀 Step 1 — Install VLC

Download VLC Media Player:

https://www.videolan.org/

Or install it from:

- Windows
- macOS
- Linux
- Android (Play Store)
- iPhone (App Store)

---

# 🚀 Step 2 — Get a Public IPTV Playlist

Example public playlists:

Sports IPTV

https://iptv-org.github.io/iptv/

Complete IPTV Collection

https://github.com/Free-TV/IPTV

---

# 🚀 Step 3 — Open VLC

Launch VLC Media Player.

Go to

Media → Open Network Stream

---

# 🚀 Step 4 — Paste Playlist URL

Paste the IPTV playlist URL.

Example:

https://iptv-org.github.io/iptv/index.m3u

Click **Play**.

---

# 🚀 Step 5 — Browse Channels

Open

View → Playlist

Choose any available channel.

---

# 🌍 Notes

- Some channels are geo-restricted.
- A VPN may be required in some countries.
- Stream quality depends on the source.
- Public playlists change frequently.

---

# ❓ Troubleshooting

### Channel not working?

Try another stream.

### Buffering?

Use a faster internet connection.

### Playlist not loading?

Verify the playlist URL is active.

### Geo-blocked?

Some streams are available only in certain countries.
